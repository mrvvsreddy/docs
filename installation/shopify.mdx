title: "Shopify Integration"
description: "Add Maedo Webchat to your Shopify store in minutes."
---

Follow these steps to integrate Maedo Webchat into your Shopify Online Store.

## Installation Steps

1.  Go to your **Shopify Admin** panel.
2.  Click on **Online Store** > **Themes**.
3.  Find your current theme and click **Customize**.
4.  In the left sidebar, scroll down to the **Footer** section.
5.  Click **Add block** (or **Add section**) and select **Custom Liquid**.
6.  Paste your Maedo embed code into the **Liquid code** box.

```html
<script defer src="https://cdn.maedo.in/scripts/webchat/widget-v2.js" data-agent-config="YOUR_AGENT_TOKEN_HERE"></script>
```

7.  Click **Save**.

## Verification

Open your Shopify storefront and verify that the chat widget appears in the bottom corner.
