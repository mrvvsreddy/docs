Maedo agents live where your customers are. Deploy a unified AI workforce that shares memory across every channel.

### Supported Channels
- **Web Store**: Embed interactive agents directly on your site to capture intent.
- **Email**: Automate personalized follow-ups that remember web context.
- **SMS & WhatsApp**: Reach users on their preferred messaging apps for price alerts and support.
