---
title: "Progressive Data Collection"
description: "Learn how Maedo builds customer profiles without intrusive forms."
---

Maedo agents use progressive data collection to learn about your customers naturally through conversation.

### Conversational Discovery
Instead of long forms, agents ask targeted questions at the right moment. "Would you like a price alert for this?" or "What's the best email to send this coupon to?"

### Persona Building
Every interaction contributes to a growing persona profile, allowing your marketing and sales teams to understand lead intent with unprecedented depth.
